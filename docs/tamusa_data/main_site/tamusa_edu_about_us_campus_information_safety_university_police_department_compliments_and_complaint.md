## Compliments & Complaints
Thank you for taking the time to provide feedback to the University Police. To submit a compliment or complaint, please follow the directions below.
### **Compliments**
Please include a compliment in your subject line.
### **Complaints**
Please include the complaint in your subject line. Our general order for processing a complaint against an officer is as follows.
**[General Order 100.13 Internal Investigations (.pdf)](https://www.tamusa.edu/about-us/campus-information/safety/university-police-department/documents/General-Order-100.13-Internal-Investigations.pdf "General Order 100.13 Internal Investigations \(.pdf\)")**
