[ Financial Wellness Financial wellness is the state of having a healthy relationship with your finances, where you can manage day-to-day expenses, save for future goals, handle unexpected financial challenges, and make informed decisions without stress. It involves budgeting, saving, understanding credit, and planning for financial stability. ](https://www.tamusa.edu/jagwellness/financial-wellness.html)
[ Spiritual Wellness Spiritual wellness involves finding purpose, meaning, and connection in life. It’s about exploring beliefs, values, and practices that provide a sense of inner peace and alignment with oneself and the world. [ Physical Wellness Physical wellness involves a healthy balance between one’s physical activity and proper nutrition. ## Mission
We promote campus-wide wellness by providing support, education, and resources, fostering an empowered and thriving community.
![](https://www.tamusa.edu/_stock-photos/academics-classroom-shots/image-teaser-1000x500/Student-Working-on-Laptop-in-Cubicle.jpg)
## Vision
A campus where holistic wellness is integral, empowering everyone to prioritize wellbeing, seek help, and thrive.
