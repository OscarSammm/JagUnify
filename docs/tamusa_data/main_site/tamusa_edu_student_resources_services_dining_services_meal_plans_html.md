## A&M-SA Dining Services Meal Plans
**Summer 2025 Meal Plans (Employee)  
** For Employees, The General 20 and General 45 meal plans are available for summer
**Commuter Student Meal Plans (AY 2025 - 2026)  
** Purchase a commuter meal plan to save time and money while dining on campus! To purchase plans for commuter students, go to JagWire > Student Services > Student Resources > GET Funds. Commuter dining dollars expire at the end of the Academic Year. Unused swipes will expire at the end of each semester. 
Commuter Student Meal Plans **Swipe 50 plan**** _Best Value!_** Cost: $490 (per semester) Meal “Swipes”: 50/semester Dining Dollars: $75  
---  
**Swipe 25 Plan** Cost: $315 (per semester) Meal “Swipes”: 25/semester Dining Dollars: $100  
**Resident Students Meal Plans (AY 2025 - 2026)  
** Students living in on-campus residence halls are required to purchase a resident meal plan. The resident meal plan is required for campus residents both fall and spring semesters. Resident students do not have to purchase their plan on the GET Funds website, they are added on the back end with their housing lease. 
Resident Student Meal Plans **Jag Unlimited Plan**** _Best Value!_** Cost: 2,350 (per semester) Meal “Swipes”: Unlimited / semester Dining Dollars: $50  
---  
**Jag 210 Plan** Cost: $1,885 (per semester) Meal “Swipes”: 210/semester Dining Dollars: $150  
**Jag 180 Plan**** _Default Plan_** Cost: $1,725 (per semester) Meal “Swipes”: 180/semester Dining Dollars: $200  
Residents, please complete the [Meal Plan Acknowledgement Form](https://tamusa.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/50/group/96/create/519).
**Employee Meal Plans (AY 2025 - 2026)**
Employee Meal Plans **General 45 Plan** Cost: $365 (per semester) Meal “Swipes”: 45  
---  
**General 20 Plan** Cost: $180 (per semester) Meal “Swipes”: 20
