## Admissions Document Submission Guidelines
### **Undergraduate Applicants**
Undergraduate applicants may submit admissions documents including high school transcripts, college transcripts, official test scores, and application fee waivers via one of the following methods:
  1. TREx-Texas Records Exchange (for Texas High Schools only)
  2. EDI-Electronic Data Interchange (for Texas Colleges and Universities only)
  3. Sent directly from the prior institution to the Admissions email: **admissions@tamusa.edu**
  4. By mail to:  
Texas A&M University-San Antonio   
Office of Admissions   
One University Way   
San Antonio, TX 78224
  5. SAT & ACT: Scores will be accepted via direct transmission from a testing agency: 
     * SAT Code: 5996
     * ACT Code: 5350
  6. Other third-party vendors that deliver official transcripts to TAMUSA include: Parchment, National Student Clearinghouse, Greenlight, Scribbles, and e-Scrip Safe.


  * NOTE: All documents submitted through the application at apply.tamusa.edu are considered unofficial. Please reference the list above for information on official document submissions.


### **Foreign Credential Evaluation Report for Undergraduate Applicants**
All foreign credential evaluation reports (course by course or document by document) should be mailed, from one of the five services below, directly to Texas A&M University San Antonio.
  1. Foreign Credentials Services of America
  2. Educational Credential Evaluators, Inc.
  3. Global Credential Evaluators, Inc.
  4. World Education Services
  5. SpanTran: The Evaluation Company


_If you are unsure of how to submit documents, or have questions about documents that are not listed, please call our Admissions Counselors at (210) 784-1300._
### **Graduate Applicants**
Graduate applicants may submit admissions documents including college transcripts, letters of degree completion/awarded, official test scores, personal statements, and resumes via one of the following methods:
  1. Request official e-transcripts from **all US institutions attended** to be sent to GradCAS through the online order portal. If your institution does not have official e-transcripts available, they can be mailed to the GradCAS processing center.**[How to send official transcripts to GradCAS](https://help.liaisonedu.com/GradCAS_Applicant_Help_Center/Sending_Your_Official_Transcripts_and_Test_Scores_to_GradCAS/Sending_Official_Transcripts_to_GradCAS "GradCAS").**
  2. All graduate admissions test score reports will be accepted via direct transmission from a testing agency.


  * Graduate Record Exam (GRE): 6712
  * Millers Analogies Test (MAT): 3013
  * Graduate Management Admissions Test (GMAT): 7B7-9D-05


### [**Sending Foreign Credential Evaluation Reports to GradCAS**](https://help.liaisonedu.com/GradCAS_Applicant_Help_Center/Sending_Your_Official_Transcripts_and_Test_Scores_to_GradCAS/Sending_Official_Transcripts_to_GradCAS/2_Foreign_and_French-Canadian_Transcripts)
