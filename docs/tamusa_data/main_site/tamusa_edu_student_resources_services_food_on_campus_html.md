Let's Eat
There are several dining locations on the Texas A&M-San Antonio campus. The new all-you-care-to-enjoy dining hall is in the Central Academic Building and includes a variety of stations for students to choose from. Students can explore a variety of stations to suit every craving, from comforting homestyle favorites to the globally inspired dishes at the Bowl Life station. They can also enjoy options at the salad bar, made-to-order deli, and a selection of sweet treats at the dessert station. Additionally, there are two on campus retail dining locations. Jaguar Java Coffee Shop is in the Science and Technology Building and offers a variety of Starbucks coffee and drinks. River Edge Coffee and Tea Bar offers Boba Tea and on the go meals inside of the Business Library Hall (BLH).
TAMUSA Dining offers a wide variety of meal plans to best suit students’ needs and to align with the transformed all-you-care-to-eat dining experience in the CAB.
[Hours of Operation](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/services/dining-services-hours-of-operation.html) [Purchase a meal plan](https://get.cbord.com/tamusa/full/login.php)

What are Meal Swipes
Meal swipes can be used at any time throughout the semester at the CAB dining hall. All the student must do is swipe once at the register, then they may enjoy unlimited servings as long as they stay in the dining hall. Unused meal swipes expire at the end of each semester. 
[Meal Plans](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/services/dining-services-meal-plans.html)
![Dining Services - French Toast](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-affairs/images/french-toast-535X400.jpg)
What are Dining Dollars
Dining Dollars work like cash and are accessible from your student ID Card. Dining Dollars are accepted at all on-campus dining locations, including food truck vendors. Unused portions of Dining Dollars carry over from the Fall to Spring semester. Dining Dollars expire at the end of the Academic Year.
[Check your Dining Dollars Balance](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/services/dining-dollars.html)
