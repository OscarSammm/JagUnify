##  Mascot 
General, the Jaguar is the Premier collegiate mascot of the Southside of San Antonio, representing the A&M University- San AntonioJaguar in competition, athletic, university, and community events.

##  Cheer & Dance 
The Jaguar Cheer is the newest competitive spirit team at A&M-San Antonio. From game day to competitions, cheer is always ready to show off our jaguar pride.
