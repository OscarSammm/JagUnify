## Emotional Wellness
Emotional wellness is the ability to manage and adapt to life’s challenges while experiencing a range of emotions in a constructive way. The more aware individuals are of their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors, the more in control they will feel managing them.

##  Student Information 
[**Student Counseling Center**](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/support/student-counseling-center/index.html) – The Student Counseling Center (SCC) provides brief individual and group therapy, crisis intervention, consultation, case management, and prevention services. These services can help support students’ academic and life goals as well as enhance their personal growth and well-being.
[**Quiet Room**](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/support/student-counseling-center/mental-health-services.html) - The Quiet Room at Texas A&M University-San Antonio is intended to provide a safe, calming, and comforting virtual environment for the A&M- SA community to learn and strengthen their coping skills. Located in Madla 120. 30 min. reservation available by walk-in or phone call at 210-784-1331. Hours: 9 A.M. to 12 P.M. | 1P.M. to 4 P.M.
[**TELUS Student Support App**](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/support/student-counseling-center/telus.html) - TELUS is an online, student support app that allows you as a student 24/7 access to professional counseling by phone or chat in multiple languages! With the TELUS app, you have access to multimedia content, health assessments, and virtual fitness. Most importantly, master's level counselors are available to you if you’re in crisis, want to connect in the moment, or want to schedule an appointment for later.
[**SCC Self-Care Packet**](https://www.canva.com/design/DAFXle2rnhc/JcCf5aCYyocOlddth3RFGw/view?utm_content=DAFXle2rnhc&utm_campaign=designshare&utm_medium=link&utm_source=viewer) – This resource has tips to manage stress and practice self-care. The packet includes coping skills, book and podcast recommendations and more!

[**Virtual Quiet Room**](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/support/student-counseling-center/mental-health-services.html) - The Virtual Quiet Room at Texas A&M University-San Antonio is intended to provide a safe, calming, and comforting virtual environment for the A&M- SA community to learn and strengthen their coping skills.
