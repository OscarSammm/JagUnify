## About the WLDCC
The Writing, Language, and Digital Composing Center offers three types of appointments! All appointments can be scheduled online through JagWire.Online Sessions: Work with a tutor in real-time from any location using voice, video, and chat! Appointments are made and accessed through our online scheduling system.
![](https://www.tamusa.edu/_stock-photos/campus-shots/hero-carousel-1000x667/Campus-Fountain.jpg)
## What We Do
The Writing Center supports students across all courses. Our writing tutors work with students to develop reading skills, prepare oral presentations, and plan, draft, and revise their written assignments. Our Spanish tutors support students composing in Spanish and completing Spanish coursework. We also support digital composing practices, whether that be creating a PowerPoint, making a podcast, or working on their digital portfolio.
![](https://www.tamusa.edu/_stock-photos/campus-shots/hero-carousel-1000x667/Bikes-Parked-on-Campus.jpg)
* * *
##  Tutoring/Etutoring Sessions 
Etutoring Sessions: Upload an assignment draft and your assignment sheet. You will receive written feedback from a tutor.
Face-to-Face Sessions: These sessions will be held in person in one of our two locations, Central Academic Building 208 or Classroom Hall 304. Please note your appointment location when scheduling in JagWire.
