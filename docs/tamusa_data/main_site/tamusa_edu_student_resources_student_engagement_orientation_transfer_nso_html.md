**Congratulations! Now that you have been admitted to Texas A &M University-San Antonio, you are ready to take the next step in your Jaguar Journey by completing Transfer Orientation.**
##  About YOUR Orientation 
It's time to meet your new university community! Orientation is an excellent opportunity for you to familiarize yourself with the university and meet your future classmates. Campus offices and departments will come together to welcome you and share important information to ensure you have a great start to your Jaguar experience.
##  Pick YOUR Orientation Date 
Once you are accepted you will be invited via email to confirm your enrollment at Texas A&M University-San Antonio. When you get the email asking you to confirm your enrollment, select YES and you'll be directed to the Enrollment Confirmation page. Complete the form and answer the required questions. Then, you'll get to select how you would like to complete transfer orientation--either in-person _**or**_ online. Summer and Fall 2025 Transfer Orientation will open March 1st and Spring 2026 will be available October 1st.
##  What YOU Will Learn 
If you opt to complete Transfer Orientation in-person you'll get to sign-up for a specific day. With the in-person option, you'll have the opportunity to explore campus, learn more about the university, and meet tons of new students just like you. You'll also get to meet faculty in your major and talk with an academic advisor about your first semester of classes! If you prefer to complete Transfer Orientation online you will get a link and will be able to complete it within 1-2 hours.
##  Why YOU Should Attend 
Completing orientation is mandatory for all transfer students before you can register for classes.
##  How YOU Can Reserve Your Spot 
Once you are accepted you will be invited via email to confirm your enrollment at Texas A&M University-San Antonio. When you get the email asking you to confirm your enrollment, select YES and you'll be directed to the Enrollment Confirmation page. Complete the form and answer the required questions. Then, you'll get to select how you would like to complete transfer orientation--either in-person or online.
##  Your Family Is Invited, Too! 
As you sign-up for New Student Orientation, don't forget to invite your family! Your mom, dad, aunts, uncles, "tios y tias," brothers, sisters, cousins, "primos y primas," and friends can take part in a special orientation just for them! The Jaguar Family Orientation program provides helpful resources and tools to help your family and friends assist you in your new Jaguar Journey!
