## Transfer Student Mentorship Program (Silver Liners)
Incoming transfer students have the opportunity to be paired with a mentor that can help them navigate their transition onto our campus. We also host an orientation for the program early on in the semester. Special transfer events are held every month where mentors & mentees and other transfer students can come and hang out, eat food, make crafts, and meet other students in the organization.
_Make sure to follow both TAMUSA.Silverliners and TAMUSA.Transfers on Instagram to get the most up to date information on transfer exclusive events!_
**Program Orientation Dates** : **Mandatory** for all mentors and mentees in the program. If schedules do not align, the makeup session or a virtual meeting can meet this requirement. All exceptions made must be approved by the lead mentor: transfer@tamusa.edu
