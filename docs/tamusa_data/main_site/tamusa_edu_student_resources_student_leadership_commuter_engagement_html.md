## Commuter Resources at A&M-San Antonio
Texas A&M University-San Antonio defines commuter students as a students who lives in anywhere other than university-owned housing. This includes students who live with family in the nearby San Antonio area or students who live independently in off-campus housing. No matter the case, we are here to help. Learn more about the resources available to commuter students below.
* * *
## Commuter Resources
##  Facilities 
### **Welcome to the Commuter Lounge!**
We know campus life looks a little different when you commute — that’s why this space was designed with you in mind. Located in the Central Academic Building, the Commuter Lounge provides a dedicated space for students who travel to campus. The Commuter Lounge offers a cozy spot to study, eat, or meet up with friends between classes. Take advantage of the free Wi-Fi, comfy seating, and microwaves that make your day easier.
Didn't bring your lunch? No problem, visit our [Dine on Campus](https://new.dineoncampus.com/tamusa) site to see what is open. You can also invest in a Commuter Student Meal Plan by going to JagWire > Student Services > Student Resources > GET Funds.
### **Extended Hours**
We get it, juggling classes and getting everything sorted is a lot. That's why we're making it easier for you! Get all your questions answered and tasks checked off your list in one convenient evening. The following offices are staying open late until 7pm on the 2nd Tuesday of every month:
  * Academic Advising
  * Academic Student Success Center
  * Bookstore
  * Cisneros Institute (Student Life, Transfer Center)
  * Disability Support Services
  * Enrollment Services Center (Financial Aid, Admissions, Registrar's Office)
  * Student Business Services
  * Mays Center
  * Military Affairs
  * Parking and Transportation


Swing by between 5pm-7pm to get the help you need without the rush. We're here to help you succeed!
##  Transportation 
### **Campus Parking Made Easy**
Finding a place to park on campus shouldn’t be stressful! Whether you’re heading to class, work, or an event, Texas A&M University–San Antonio offers a variety of parking options to fit your needs. From student permits to hourly parking, you can explore available lots, permits, and shuttle routes online.
Learn more about [parking](https://resources.tamusa.edu/employees/business-affairs/parking-transportation/index.html) at A&M–San Antonio.
### **Other Transportation Options Through VIA**
  * [VIA UPass – Unlimited Bus Rides](https://www.viainfo.net/Upass/)
  * [VIA Link - Rideshare](https://www.viainfo.net/link/)


##  Annual Programs 
### **Celebrate Our First Commuter Student Week!**
Join us for the **inaugural Commuter Student Week: Going the Distance** at Texas A&M University–San Antonio, happening **March 16–20, 2026!** This week is all about celebrating and supporting our commuter Jaguars with events, resources, and opportunities to connect. Enjoy free snacks, giveaways, and activities designed to make your campus experience even better—no matter where your commute begins.
Stay tuned for the full schedule and get ready to celebrate the Jaguars who go the extra mile!
