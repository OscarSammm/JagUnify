# TAMUSA Esports
  

  

  

## Overview
Our mission is to create a supportive gaming community at Texas A&M University - San Antonio that operates at the highest standards through competition and service. With dedicated staff and facilities we uphold the institution's education values while encouraging personal and academic growth of student gamers.  
  
The Department of Recreational Sports has brought varsity esports to campus in the Spring of 2020. Our esports program is designed to promote gaming as a beneficial activity for students in the A&M– San Antonio community and beyond.
