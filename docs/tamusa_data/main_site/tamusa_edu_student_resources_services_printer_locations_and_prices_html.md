### General Use Printers - Bring your Jaguar Pass to Print
**Each semester students receive $25.00**  
Any remaining balance of the $25 allowance does not carry over to the next semester. Students must pay for all of their printing or copying charges once their $25 allowance is exhausted. [Click here](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/services/jaguar-id-card/cash-plan.html) to learn more about how to add more money to your Jaguar Pass.
