If you are a **new** A&M student your Jaguar username should be a combination of your name and numbers (e.g. John Smith- jsmit034).   

If you are a **returning** student or **alumni** your Jaguar username can begin with a K followed by 8 numbers (e.g. K12345678)
Your Jaguar password is the password you created during your first-time login. 
** NOTE   
** **If you have never signed in to Jagwire before or created a password, click here for your[first time login](https://www.tamusa.edu/Information-Technology-Services/student-first-time-login-assistance.html) **.   
** If you do not have a temporary password or are having issues with the temporary password that was provided to you. Please contact the helpdesk at 210-784-4357 (HELP). **
Your Jaguar **username** and **password** is the account you will use to login to computers on campus, connect to campus wireless services, access Library databases remotely, login to JagWire, Blackboard, and Jaguar email. 
To **[reset or unlock your account](https://selfservice.tamusa.tamus.edu/showLogin.cc) **. If you do not know your Jaguar username, please contact the **Welcome Center** at (210) 784-1300. 
## **Accessing Jaguar Email & Office 365**
  1. Visit & log in to: **<https://jagwire.tamusa.edu/> **
  2. Click on the email icon in the left-hand pane
    1. Username: jaguarusername@jaguar.tamu.edu 
    2. Password: Jaguar Password


Your Jaguar email is a Microsoft Exchange account that also allows you to access the Microsoft Office 365 applications for FREE!
Instructions on how to **[install Office 365](https://www.tamusa.edu/Information-Technology-Services/documents/Install-O365-Flyer.pdf) **. 
### **Blackboard Account Information**
Your Blackboard login is the same as your login for Jagwire. You can access Blackboard through the following ways:
  1. Log in to Jagwire: **<https://jagwire.tamusa.edu/> ** and click on Blackboard 
  2. Visit Blackboard directly and login: **<https://tamusa.blackboard.com> **


If you still have questions about accessing Blackboard, click here or contact the ITS Help Desk.
### **JagWire FAQs**
Access the **[FAQ Sheet](https://www.tamusa.edu/Information-Technology-Services/documents/Updated-Jagwire-FAQ.pdf) ** for JagWire for frequently asked questions. 
If you have any more questions or account issues, please contact the **ITS Help Desk** at (210) 784-4357 or submit a service request from our **[homepage](https://www.tamusa.edu/Information-Technology-Services/index.html) **.
