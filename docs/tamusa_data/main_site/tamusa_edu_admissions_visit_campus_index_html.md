## There are many ways to visit Texas A&M University-San Antonio
## Special Events for Future Jaguars
Welcome to Texas A&M University-San Antonio! We are excited to have you join us on campus and virtually to explore your opportunities as a future Jaguar!
[Future Jaguar Day](https://www.tamusa.edu/admissions/visit-campus/special-events/future-jaguar-day.html) [Virtual Info Sessions](https://www.tamusa.edu/admissions/visit-campus/special-events/paying-for-college.html)
![Jaguar March](https://www.tamusa.edu/about-us/news/images/2023/10/Jaguar-March.jpg)
## A&M-SA Preview Day
Join A&M-San Antonio as we visit community colleges across San Antonio, Texas! Our admissions counselors are coming to your campus to discuss how you can seamlessly transfer to A&M-San Antonio.
**Dates are to be announced.**
## Application Workshop Series (Transfer and Returning Students)
Take the next step toward becoming a Jaguar! The Application Workshop Series connects you with our Admissions Team and gets you personalized support through every stage of the admissions process.
Whether you prefer an in-person experience, a community-based setting or the convenience of a virtual session, there’s an option for you. 
**Dates are to be announced.**
Visit San Antonio
As the 7th largest city in the nation, San Antonio is full of exciting opportunities to embrace the rich culture of this historic city! Visit San Antonio to get a first-hand experience of all that the city has to offer our Jaguar community.
