## Future Jaguar Day 2026
Experience what it means to be a Jaguar at Texas A&M University-San Antonio’s Future Jaguar Day! This special event gives future Jaguars and their families the chance to connect one-on-one with Admission Counselors and Financial Aid Advisers, explore scholarship opportunities and learn about ways to fund your education.
Plus, you'll get to tour our beautiful campus and see firsthand what makes A&M-San Antonio the perfect place for your academic journey.
9 am to 1:30 pm
March 28, 2026
[RSVP Here](https://visit.tamusa.edu/event/tamusa.events.1158736)

## Plan Your Visit
##  What can I expect from attending Future Jaguar Day? 
At Future Jaguar Day at Texas A&M University-San Antonio, you and your family can experience an exciting and informative day designed to help you explore life as a Jaguar. From connecting with admission counselors to touring campus, this event gives you the chance to picture yourself as part of the Jaguar family and see firsthand what makes A&M-San Antonio the perfect place to start your journey!
##  Are parking passes required? 
No, parking permits are not required for this event. Guests are welcome to park in Lot #2, Lot #3 or in the City Lanes during the event. It is important to park only in these designated areas, as parking elsewhere may result in a citation.
##  When will I receive final details for the event? 
A final reminder email will be sent before the event, including details on the start time, parking instructions, campus map and more.
##  Where do I go once I arrive at Future Jaguar Day? 
Once you arrive at Future Jaguar Day, you will check-in at the Recreational Center. From there, you will receive a map to help guide you through the campus so that you can visit all of the services available to you.
##  Who can I reach out to if I'm lost or have questions? 
You can contact the Enrollment Services Center at (210) 784-1300 or email us at BeAJaguar@tamusa.edu
