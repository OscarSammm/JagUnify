## Environmental Wellness
Environmental wellness is the ability to recognize one’s responsibility for the quality of the environment surrounding us, promotes interaction with nature and one’s personal environment, and encourages all to practice habits that promote a healthy environment and a more balanced lifestyle. 
![Environmental Wellness](https://www.tamusa.edu/jagwellness/images/Environmental-Wellness.png)

##  Employee 
**[Improving Environmental Wellness in the Workplace](https://www.wellics.com/blog/environmental-wellness-in-the-workplace)** - Environmental wellness in the workplace is a crucial factor that directly impacts employee health, productivity, and overall job satisfaction. When employees work in spaces that harmoniously blend natural elements, proper lighting, ergonomic design, and clean air quality, they experience enhanced physical and mental well-being.
A thoughtfully designed workplace environment serves as a foundation for professional success and personal comfort. The physical surroundings in which employees spend their working hours significantly influence their ability to perform tasks effectively, collaborate with colleagues, and maintain positive mental health.
