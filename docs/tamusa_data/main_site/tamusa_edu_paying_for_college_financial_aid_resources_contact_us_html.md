## Processing & Response Timelines
Our team is committed to reviewing documents and responding to inquiries as quickly as possible. Please review the timelines below to help guide your planning. You can also check your financial aid and document status through your [JagApp](http://jagwire.tamusa.edu/ "JagApp Portal") account.
We appreciate your patience during busy periods. If your processing time exceeds the guidelines below, feel free to contact us.
##  Inquiries and Emails 
  * **Standard Timeframe:** 1-2 business days
  * **Peak Periods (e.g., payment deadlines, refunds):** Up to 3 business days


##  Processing 
  * **FAFSA:** 3-5 business days to receive files
  * **eTASFA:** 3-5 business days to receive files
  * **TASFA Awarding:** 7-10 business days from submission
  * **Verification Documents:** 5-7 business days from the most recent submission
  * **SAP Appeals:** 5-7 business days from the most recent submission
  * **Professional Judgment Appeals (PJs):** 5-7 business days after  _all_ documents are submitted.


##  Loans 
  * **Direct Loans:** 7-10 business days from acceptance (ensure EC/MPN is completed)
  * **Direct PLUS Loans (Parent & Graduate):** 7-10 business days from acceptance (ensure EC/MPN is completed)
  * **Master Promissory Note/Entrance Counseling/Exit Counseling:** 3-5 business days to receive files
  * **Alternative Loans:** 7-10 business days after application
