Texas A&M University-San Antonio values student development, and through the Office of Student Life, provides fulfilling on-campus experiences that can help students reach their full potential. The The Office of Student Life focuses on student experience, including activities, events, student organizations, University-sponsored groups, lectures, live performances, special events and more. Our goal is to get students engaged on campus, and we employ professional and student guides available to help them achieve their academic and personal goals.
![Connect Four](https://www.tamusa.edu/_stock-photos/events-campus-life/image-teaser-1000x500/Connect-Four.jpg)
**Learn more about what we offer by visiting JagSync, calling us at (210) 784-1329, or stopping by the office.**
[Visit JagSync](https://jagsync.tamusa.edu/)
![Jaguar Cheerleaders with Torre Pinata](https://www.tamusa.edu/_stock-photos/events-campus-life/image-teaser-1000x500/Jaguar-Cheerleaders-with-Torre-Pinata.jpg)
JagSync provides information about student organizations, campus events, recreational sports and more. Student organizations have access to policies and procedures, University forms and group rosters. Within JagSync, student groups can complete University forms, reserve spaces and request approval for any on-campus event.
