## Program Goals
Students participating in the PROWL program will 
  * Have access to a Peer Leader who will transition them into a career pathway through a connection to experiential learning opportunities;
  * Increase their wellness through connections to campus resources;
  * Create community and build relationships through social experiences on and off campus;
  * Be assisted in finishing out the first year and enrolling for their second year at the institution;
  * Learn to advocate for themselves to cultivate academic, professional, and personal growth.
  * **Applying for the PROWL Pathway Scholarship? There will be a recommendation form to provide to your recommended. Instead of a letter of recommendation, we ask that they submit the recommendation form when the it becomes available.**


## Career Connections
The Peer Leaders will support their students in making further 
connections on campus that align with their career goals they will be connected to:
  * Internships
  * Career Advisor Appointments
  * Student Organizations
  * Leadership Opportunities
  * Faculty-led Research Projects
  * Departmental Initiatives
  * Experimental Learning
  * Study Abroad Course
  * Faculty and Staff Mentorship


Pathways Database opportunities can be found within the PROWL [Blackboard](https://tamusa.blackboard.com/)

## First-Year Cording Ceremony
At the end of the academic year, the Office of First-Year Experience will host a First-Year Cording Ceremony to celebrate the accomplishments of first-year students and their involvement in PROWL. Students will be able to receive the following recognitions.
  * Students who earn a C or higher for First-Year Seminar and connected with a Peer Leader (at minimum) two out of the four PROWL activities, will be awarded a graduation cord and certificate.
  * A campus wide request will be sent out to nominate students for first-year awards.


![Spr 2025 Cording Ceremony Group Photo](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/first-year-experience/PROWL/Images/sp25_cording_group_photo.jpeg)
## PROWL Pathway Scholarship
The Office of First-Year Experience has created a scholarship fund to award students who have been actively part of PROWL and demonstrated growth in their first-year A&M-SA. This is a self-nominated scholarship; however, successful nominees must meet the scholarship requirements. There will be a total of 10, one-time $500 scholarships awarded to qualifying nominees in Fall 2025.
  * Requirements
    * Earn a C or higher for First-Year Seminar in the Fall of 2025
    * 2.5 GPA or higher
    * Connected with Peer Leader (at minimum) two out of the four PROWL activities
    * Demonstrated personal growth during their first year at A&M-SA
    * Submit a recommendation form from a Faculty, Staff, or Peer Leader
  * Scholarships will be based on attendance and growth in student’s first-year
  * Applications will open in April 2026.


More information about the PROWL Pathways Scholarship can be found on Scholarship Universe once the applications open.
Applying for the PROWL Pathway Scholarship? The Prowl Pathway Scholarship forms will be made available soon.
