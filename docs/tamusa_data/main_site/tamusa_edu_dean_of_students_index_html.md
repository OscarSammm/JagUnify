## Your Success is our Mission
The Dean of Students Office fosters the development of civility, accountability, and responsibility; builds community through service and involvement; and serves as an advocate for all students. This office is dedicated to supporting the students who need assistance resolving complex personal and academic matters. We strive to help students achieve their goals and enhance the student experience.
![](https://www.tamusa.edu/dean-of-students/images/DOS-Grad-Success.png)
## Greetings from the Dean of Students
![Zeak Naifeh](https://www.tamusa.edu/dean-of-students/images/zeak-naifeh.jpg)
Welcome to Texas A&M San Antonio! As the Dean of Students, I am pleased to assist you in your academic, personal, and professional development as you navigate life in and outside the classroom. The Dean of Students team is staffed with experienced, caring, and talented individuals dedicated to assuring a dynamic and fulfilling college experience.I encourage you to get involved in clubs and organizations, participate in various university programs, and, when needed, utilize all the support resources available. Your university experience should be well rounded, as learning goes much beyond the classroom, regardless of where it takes place - whether in a study group, orientation, club meeting, or one-on-one support meeting.
  
Students often are unclear where and when to turn to for help when something arises. If you need help along this journey, contact our office to help you with any questions and concerns. We offer various assistance such as medical concerns, financial resources, community resources, and much more!  
  
I look forward to seeing you around campus, and don't hesitate to ask for our assistance! Please stop by offices or call any time to speak to someone about your interests, concerns, suggestions, or questions. We want to help you make the most of your college experience; we need to hear from you.
Go Jags!
Zeak T. Naifeh, Ph.D.  
Associate Vice President and Dean of Students
