## Regional Receptions 2025
**We’re Bringing Jaguar Pride to You!**
Texas A&M University-San Antonio is hitting the road, and we’re coming to **McAllen, Houston, Austin, and Laredo**! Our **Regional Receptions** are a special opportunity for admitted first-year and transfer students to connect with the Jaguar community and learn what it means to be part of A&M-San Antonio.
Don’t miss your chance to experience the Jaguar spirit firsthand! **Space is limited,** so be sure to **register today** and save your spot.

## Frequently Asked Questions
##  Who can attend the Regional Receptions? 
These events are open to admitted students, as well as one additional guests.
##  Is there a cost to attend? 
No, the Regional Receptions are **free** for all attendees.
##  What should I expect at the event? 
At the event, you will connect with other incoming Jaguars, hear from A&M-San Antonio representatives who will provide valuable insights and guidance for your upcoming college experience and enjoy a delectable dinner, courtesy of A&M-San Antonio.
##  Who can I contact for more information? 
You can contact the Enrollment Services Center at (210) 784-1300 or email us at BeAJaguar@tamusa.edu
