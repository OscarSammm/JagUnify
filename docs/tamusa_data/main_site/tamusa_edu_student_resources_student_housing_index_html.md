First-Year Student Live-On Requirement
First-year students whose permanent address is more than 40 miles away must live on campus. Students living within 40 miles of the university may submit a request for exemption, but the request does not automatically exempt the student. Students who graduate from high school immediately before beginning at the university are considered first-year students and fall under the first-year live-on requirement.

## On-Campus Housing Process
![number-1-icon.png](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/student-housing/images/number-1-icon.png)
Application
  

You've received notice that you have been admitted to the university. Now it is time to submit your housing application.

After Roomsync closes, we complete placements and assignments go out in the summer.

##  Housing Eligibility 
**New, Non-Continuing Students (new transfer students, international graduate students, first time at A &M-San Antonio but not a First-Year student) may also be housed on campus.**
**To be eligible for housing, all first-year and new, non-continuing students must:**
  *     * be admitted to A&M-San Antonio,

##  Contracts and Roommate Matching 
**Contracts**
2. Contracts go to the student's email if they are 18 or older. The parent or guardian of the student must sign the contract if the student is under 18.
4. Contracts are binding contracts for the academic year (August move-in to May move-out). Students wishing to cancel their contracts early are subject to a fee schedule and, depending on the timing, may be subject to all housing fees for the term. Requests to terminate may be approved or denied. **Submitting a Request to Terminate, moving out, and returning keys DOES NOT cancel the contract without prior approval.** Please ensure that costs are considered and the contract is read in its entirety before signing, as it is a binding agreement. 
**Roommate Selection/Matching**
  1. Roommate Selection/Matching is not available to students without a signed and executed contract for housing. 
  2. Students will receive an email from RoomSync within 48 hours of signing their housing contract.
  3. Students will self-select their roommate based on the information provided and available in RoomSync.
  4. If a student signs their contract on or after June 30th or does not make their roommate selection, Housing staff will place the student based on the information in RoomSync regarding preferences and compatibility.


##  Important Dates 
  *     * February 2, 2026-May 31, 2026- Application open for ** _ALL_**  _**NEW**_ _students_
      * _Transfers, graduate students, international students_
      * _FTIC living greater than 40 miles from campus_
    * June 1, 2026- Open to**All** students until filled
      * Leasing is first come, first served
      * Must be enrolled full-time or signed up for NSO
      * Must have a 2.0 GPA or higher (continuing students)
      * Must be in good academic standing (continuing students)
      * August 3, 2025- Last day to register for full-time enrollment
    * **August 4, 2026-** Unregistered students will be moved onto a waitlist until their registration commitments are fulfilled. Housing is not guaranteed for those who are waitlisted and the previous housing option contracted for and roommates selected may no longer be available. 


##  Residential Meal Plan Information 
All students living in on-campus housing are required to have a meal plan. In very rare cases, an exemption may be granted. Understand that signing a lease with Student Housing contractually binds you to have a meal plan at the designated amount already determined. The meal plan consists of meal swipes for the all you can eat dining hall and dining dollars for retails locations on campus.
