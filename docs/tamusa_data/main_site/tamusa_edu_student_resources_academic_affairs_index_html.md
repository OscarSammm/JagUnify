## A New Tradition Begins with You
Texas A&M University-San Antonio provides every student the opportunity to learn in an accessible and collaborative environment. Our beautiful campus on the south side of San Antonio is only paralleled by the dedication of our faculty and staff to serving our students and providing a pathway for all to achieve their dreams of a better future through education.
![](https://www.tamusa.edu/_stock-photos/commencement/image-teaser-1000x500/Graduates-with-Maracas.jpg)
## Office of the Provost
The Office of the Provost fosters and supports student success and academic excellence. The provost's office coordinates maintains the academic programs offered at A&M- San Antonio and develops new programs. The provost guides the university’s academic division to achieve President Ochoa's vision of continuing to be one of the fastest-growing universities in Texas while balancing access, affordability, and excellence in all we do.
