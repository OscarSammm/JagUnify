## Types of Trainings
Texas A&M University-San Antonio offers CliftonStrengths individual coaching sessions and group workshops for the community. We encourage you to complete the Facilitation Request form below if you're interested in Strengths training, and we will follow up with you.
**Individual Coaching**
CliftonStrengths is a worldwide popular and effective tool for enhancing personal, social, and professional growth:
  * 3x more likely to report having an excellent quality of life
  * 6x more likely to be engaged in school/job
  * 8% increase in productivity

  

Led by Gallup Certified coaches, the Cisneros Institute for Emerging Leaders at Texas A&M University-San Antonio will help you discover how to leverage your Top 5 strengths in your personal life and profession.
The Individual Coaching series includes three one-hour sessions. Additional coaching sessions can be added and personalized based on need and interest.
  * CS 101: Discover Strengths
  * CS 201: Lead with Strengths 
  * CS 301: Maximize Strengths


**Individual Coaching Session**
  * Online assessment for Top 5 Strengths
  * Electronic CliftonStrengths Top 5 Report
  * Three facilitated sessions with Strengths Coach
  * Strengths overview
  * Personalized activities and handouts for continued learning


**Group Facilitated Strength Sessions**
CliftonStrengths is a popular and effective tool for enhancing personal and professional growth and team-building at non-profits, small businesses, and corporations.
Through a facilitated workshop led by a Gallup Certified CliftonStrengths Coach, the Cisneros Institute for Emerging Leaders at A&M-SA will elevate your professional development to the next level. 
CliftonStrengths has proven the following results:
  * 72% lower attrition in high-turnover organizations
  * 15% higher employee engagement
  * 8% increase in productivity


Group session topics and duration will be tailored to client needs. 
**Example of 1-hour Group Session**
  * Online Assessment for Top 5 Strengths (1 hour) taken before workshop
  * Electronic CliftonStrengths Top 5 Report
  * Strengths Overview
  * Name it! Claim it! Aim it! Activity
  * Networking Bingo Activity


For questions, please email us at cisneros@tamusa.edu
