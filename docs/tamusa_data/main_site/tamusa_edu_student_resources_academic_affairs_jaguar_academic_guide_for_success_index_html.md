## LEAP Program
The LEAP program supports A&M-San Antonio students whose institutional GPA. has fallen below 2.0. The program provides individualized coaching sessions to improve study habits, identifies and removes obstacles that hinder success, and determines appropriate resources based on student needs. 
###  **Program Benefits**
**Program Benefits** Individualized Academic Coaching sessions.  
---  
Awareness of campus-wide academic resources and/or community resources.  
Opportunity to improve academic strategies and tools for success.  
Institutional GPA. increase with marked success to move back into good standing.  
* * *
### **Program Requirements**
Program participation is required for students on **[Scholastic Probation](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/Academic-Success-Center/academic-policies/index.html "Scholastic Probation") **or for students receiving an **approved[ Academic Reinstatement Appeal](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/Academic-Success-Center/academic-policies/academic-reinstatement-appeal-form.html "Academic Reinstatement Appeal")**.
Designed with academic success in mind, program requirements for each semester include:
**Program Requirements** 1. |  Attend **ONE (1) LEAP Program Information session** (students may not begin program actions until they have completed their info session).  
---|---  
2. |  Complete**(1)Required appointment** with your Academic Success Coach ( virtual, face to face or by phone)**.**  
3. |  Complete  **TWO****(2) LEAP Program Actions**  
+ Time management activity  
+ Attend G.R.O.W.L. Series Workshops  - academic enhanced workshops   
  
  
  
4. | A **2.0 minimum term GPA _._** is required for students to continue enrollment for the next term. _(see[**Academic Standing minimum grade requirements**](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/Academic-Success-Center/academic-policies/index.html "Academic Standing")) _  
**NOTE:** Students will have a **registration hold** ( _Student Success Hold_) placed on their account until all probation requirements have been met for each semester. _Therefore, failure to complete LEAP program requirements for each semester will prevent students from registering for the following semester._
