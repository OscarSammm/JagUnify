**2025 Jaguar Awards**
The Jaguar Awards recognize leadership and involvement in the A&M-SA community. The Office of Student Involvement hosted this year's awards. Still, they included awards from Family Engagement, Transfer Student Engagement, Recreational Sports, Office of First-Year Experience, and the Mays Center for Experiential Learning & Community Engagement. 
For 2021-2022, 44 recognized student organizations hosted over 1,500 events. From tabling on campus to guest speakers, community service projects, fundraisers, cultural events, bible studies, educational and political forums and workshops, food, clothing, toy, and book drives, and your general meetings – in-person and virtual! We also saw a variety of co-hosted events with each other and departments! This year, we have had some collaborations with offices such as Military Affairs, The Student Counseling Center, Student Involvement, Recreational Sports, The Mays Center, the University Library, and many more!
