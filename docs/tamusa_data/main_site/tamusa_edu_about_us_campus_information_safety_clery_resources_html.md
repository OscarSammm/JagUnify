## Clery Resources
**Department of Education Clery Resources Handbook**
This document provides guidelines for publishing the Annual Security Report for Colleges and Universities.
**[Clery Handbook](http://www2.ed.gov/admins/lead/safety/handbook.pdf "Clery Handbook")**
**Campus Security Authority Form**
This form is used for Faculty and Staff designated as Campus Security Authorities. 
**[Campus Security Authority Form](https://www.tamusa.edu/about-us/campus-information/safety/university-police-department/crime-statistic-report-form.html "Campus Security Authority Form")**
**Notice of Victim Rights and Options**
When an alleged Clery Act violation occurs, victims are entitled to receive information regarding the procedures to be followed if a crime of dating violence, domestic violence, sexual assault, or stalking has occurred, including written information about their rights and options.
  * **[Victim Rights and Options Pamphlet Full Version](https://www.tamusa.edu/about-us/campus-information/safety/university-police-department/documents/Victims-Rights-and-Options-Pamphlet-Full-Version-2024.pdf "Victims Rights and Options Pamphlet Full Version")**
  * **[Victim Rights and Options Pamphlet Short Version - English (Print Friendly)](https://www.tamusa.edu/about-us/campus-information/safety/university-police-department/documents/Victim_Rights_and_Options_Short_Version.pdf "Victims Rights and Options Pamphlet Short Version - English \(Print Friendly\)") **
  * **[Victim Rights and Options Pamphlet Short Version - Spanish (Print Friendly)](https://www.tamusa.edu/about-us/campus-information/safety/university-police-department/documents/NORO-Victim-Rights-and-Options-Brochure-Spanish.pdf "Victims Rights and Options Pamphlet Short Version - Spanish \(Print Friendly\)")**


**Mandatory Reporting to Title IX Coordinator**
Senate Bill 212 amended the Texas Education Code to require an employee of a public or private, or independent higher education who witnesses or receives information regarding incidents of sexual harassment, sexual assault, dating violence or stalking must report to it to the Title IX Coordinator.
A CSA’s report to the Title IX Coordinator will fulfill Clery Act VAWA statistical reporting requirements for these incidents of crime.
**[TITLE IX](https://www.tamusa.edu/university-policies/titleix/index.html "TITLE IX")**
