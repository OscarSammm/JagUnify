## Get Ready for the Upcoming Semester with FYE and Academic Advising
The Pre-Registration Party is your one-stop shop for getting pre-registered for the upcoming semester and clearing up any Holds on your account. All first-year students are invited to this exciting event where you can set yourself up for a successful semester ahead!
**If You’re Taking First-Year Seminar:**
Great news! You’ll be attending the Pre-Registration Party with your class during your regular class time. There’s no need to RSVP or register separately for this event—just come prepared to get everything sorted out with your First-Year Seminar Class.
**PROWL Students:**
If you are currently a PROWL student, you will be sent a form to fill out and select a date/time! If you have any questions, please reach out to your Peer Leader for more information. 
Upcoming Event Details:
**Dates:** Tuesday, March 4, 2025 - Wednesday, March 5, 2025
**Location:** Vista Room (CAB 4th floor)
**What to Expect:**
**Group Advising:** Meet with Academic Advisors and get personalized guidance.
**Campus Resources:** Access to various campus offices and departments to resolve any Holds on your account.
**Swag & Surprises:** You might even win some fun prizes and swag!
Mark your calendar and get ready to start planning for the next semester. We look forward to seeing you at the Pre-Registration Party! 
![Pre-reg event](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/first-year-experience/images/PreReg-Event-image.JPG)
Contact Information
For more information, keep an eye on your email and our website. If you have any questions, feel free to contact FYE@TAMUSA.EDU.
