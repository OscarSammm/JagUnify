## Our Mission
Our mission is to act as a catalyst for student veteran success by providing resources, network support and advocacy to, through, and beyond higher education.
[Learn More](https://studentveterans.org/about/)
![SVA Logo Left Teaser](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/military-affairs/images/SVA-Logo-Image-Alternating.png)
SVA Elections will happen October 3rd through 10th on Jagsync.
## What is SVA?
Student Veterans of America (SVA) is the premier organization leading service, research, and advocacy for veterans and higher education.  
  
We elevate the academic, professional, and personal development of veterans in higher education through chapter programs and services, outcomes and impacts research, and advocacy at every level. Through a dedicated network of more than 1,500 on-campus chapters in all 50 states, and three countries overseas representing more than 750,000 student veterans, SVA inspires yesterday's warriors by connecting student veterans with a community of like-minded chapter leaders.   
  
Every day, we work to provide the necessary resources, network support, and advocacy to ensure student veterans can effectively connect, expand their skills, and ultimately achieve their greatest potential.
## FAQ
##  How to Join 
Joining is simple! Log into [Jagsync](https://tamusa.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/student-veterans-of-america) and look up Student Veterans of America, once you have found our organization then you will be able to join. There are no fees to join - just a willingness to connect, participate, and support fellow student veterans.
##  Who can Join 
Membership is open to ALL military-affiliated students, including veterans, active duty, national guard, reservists, ROTC cadets, and military family members. Allies who support the veteran community are also welcome to join. 
##  Meeting Times 
Meeting details are posted on our social media and emailed to members who have joined through [Jagsync](https://tamusa.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/student-veterans-of-america). Remember to follow us, so that you never miss out.
##  Member Benefits 
  * Build community with fellow student veterans and allies
  * Networking and mentorship opportunities
  * Access to career workshops, resume help, and employer connections
  * Leadership and volunteer opportunities on campus and within the community
  * Support, advocacy, and resources tailed to student veterans
