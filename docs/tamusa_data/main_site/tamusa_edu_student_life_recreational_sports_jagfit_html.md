Fitness & Wellness 
Recreational Sports, our philosophy behind fitness & wellness takes root in our System of Pillars that we view as our foundation for successful change.
Our Pillars of Success that build our JagFit Program Foundation include
  * Consistency
  * Hard Work
  * Learning
  * Resilience


_**Welcome to our Jag Fit Program**_
![](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-life/recreational-sports/jagfit.html)

## Programs 
##  JagFit Training 
Rec Sports offers 
Personal One-On-One Training through different packages
Consists of 4 sessions/month
Consists of 8 sessions/month
Consists of 12 sessions/month
Combine Package of Training & Coaching 
These packages consist of the following 
  * Free Initial Consultation
  * Personal Workout Plans
  * Access to our Jagfit band app for check-ins and communication


[Get started here](https://calendly.com/msantos-tamusa/trainingconsultation)
For more information, contact the Fitness & Wellness Coordinator
msantos@tamusa.edu
##  JagFit Coaching 
Rec Sports offers 
Personal One-On-One Nutritional Coaching through different packages
Consists of 2 sessions/month
Consists of 3 sessions/month
Consists of 4 sessions/month
Combine Package of Training & Coaching 
These packages consist of the following 
  * Free Initial Consultation
  * Coaching session
  * Follow Up 
  * Access to our Jagfit band app for check-ins and communication


[Get started here](https://calendly.com/msantos-tamusa/trainingconsultation)
For more information reach contact the Fitness & Wellness Coordinator
msantos@tamusa.edu

##  Equipment Orientation 
**Rec Sports offers the following**
**Equipment Orientation for the Fitness Center**
This orientation consists of a Fitness Center Tour and an introduction session. Staff/Faculty need a Rec Sports Membership to use this JagFit Program. 
Sign up for this program, and the JagFit team will get back to you within 24- 48 hours. To set up a meeting for scheduling and more information.
