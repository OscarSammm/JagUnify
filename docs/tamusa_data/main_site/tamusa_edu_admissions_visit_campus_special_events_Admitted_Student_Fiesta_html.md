## Admitted Student Fiesta
**You're in! Welcome to Texas A &M University-San Antonio, future Jaguar! **
Get ready to celebrate your incredible achievement at our fiesta-themed ceremony, where the fun begins and your Jaguar journey officially kicks off! All invited students will receive a special Jaguar medallion—your first symbol of Jaguar pride!
We can’t wait to celebrate with you! See you there, and go Jaguars!
## Spring 2025
This exclusive event is for admitted students only. Those who have been admitted to Texas A&M University-San Antonio will receive an email invitation to join us for Admitted Student Fiesta.
![Future Jaguar Student](https://www.tamusa.edu/admissions/visit-campus/images/Future-Jaguar-Student.png)
* * *
## Frequently Asked Questions
##  Who can attend the Admitted Student Fiesta? 
Admitted Student Fiesta is an invite-only event exclusively for admitted students. If you’ve received an invitation, check your email for all the event details and how to RSVP. We can’t wait to celebrate with you!
##  Do I need to register to attend? 
Yes! Registration is required to ensure we can provide the best experience for you and your guests.
##  Where will the event be held? 
The Admitted Student Fiesta will be hosted on the Texas A&M University-San Antonio campus. Specific location details and parking instructions will be provided upon registration.
##  What should I bring? 
We recommend bringing comfortable shoes for walking and any questions you have about becoming a Jaguar. Be ready to celebrate and capture memories!
##  Who can I contact for more information? 
You can contact the Enrollment Services Center at (210) 784-1300 or email us at BeAJaguar@tamusa.edu
