## Public Safety Systems
Public Safety Systems supports the departmental use of designated systems by researching, recommending, coordinating, implementing, and/or managing systems and software utilized for public safety and police operations. This office also oversees the operations of the University Police Department's Communications Center.
In addition to managing the SafeZone campus safety App and the JAG-E ALERT emergency notification system, this office provides coordination for the following systems:
  * Security cameras
  * Security and duress alarms
  * Emergency blue light phones (E-Phones)
  * Access control 


In accordance with approved plans, CPTED survey findings, physical security principles, loss prevention requirements, and business case criteria.

##  Jag-E Alert 
### A&M-SA Police Department Emergency Notification  
---  
[Jag-E Alert PDF Instructions for Registration](https://www.tamusa.edu/about-us/campus-information/safety/university-police-department/documents/JagE-Alert-Instructions-For-Registration1.pdf)  
[Jag-E Alert Emergency Notification System Login](https://eei.tamusa.edu/samlsso?spEntityID=sso.blackboardconnect.com)  
In addition to using the web-based system to manage your contact information, you can download the JagE Alert app (Blackboard MyConnect) for your iPhone (Android app currently not available) and activate it using your official University email.  
  
**Emergency Notifications:**  
  
During an emergency situation, information will be released to the University community through the JagE Alert System. This is a system that quickly notifies registered users of campus emergencies through text messages, emails and/or voicemail alerts. These are issued through the Emergency portion of the JagE Alert system.  
  
**Examples of when an Emergency Notification could be sent include (but not limited to):**
  * Active Shooter
  * Bomb Threat
  * Pandemic
  * Fire
  * Evacuations


**Timely Warnings:**  
In the event a situation arises on campus that, in the judgment of the Chief of A&M-SA PD or designee, constitutes a serious or continuous threat, a university‐wide “timely warning” will be issued. Timely Warnings are issued through email only using the Outreach portion of the JagE Alert System.  
  
**Timely Warnings are issued for Clery Reportable Crimes and may include:**
  * Sexual Assault
  * Burglaries
  * Fondling
  * Hate Crimes


**Other topics that could be sent using Outreach:**
  * Weather Closures
  * Parking Lot closures
  * Exercise/Drill Notifications
