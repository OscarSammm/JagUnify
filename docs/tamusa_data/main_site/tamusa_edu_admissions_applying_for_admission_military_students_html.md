Welcome to A&M-SA
To get started using military benefits at A&M-SA, please see the steps listed below. You are considered a new benefit user if you are using benefits at A&M-SA for the first time. This includes first-year students, transfer students, and students switching benefits. If you need assistance navigating the process, please contact our office.
[Submit Benefit Request](https://bit.ly/3rzmICL)
![](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/military-affairs/images/military-General-the-Jaguar.jpg)
1. University Application process 
The first step is applying for admission. For Undergraduate and Graduate application steps, please visit our Office of Admissions.
[Admissions](https://www.tamusa.edu/admissions/index.html)
2. Verify Military Benefits 
Know which benefits you qualify for and what they do so you can use them. View all VA benefits at va.gov. For Hazlewood information, visit /www.tvc.texas.gov
[Benefit Checklists](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/military-affairs/VA-Education-Benefits.html)
3. Military Benefits Process 
While applying to the university, you may need to apply for your military benefits. To apply for federal VA education benefits, visit the VA webpage.
[VA.gov](https://www.va.gov/)
4. Orientation 
Once you have been accepted to A&M-SA, you must attend New Student Orientation or Transfer Orientation.
[Orientation](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/student-engagement/orientation/index.html)
5. Registration 
After completing your Orientation, you will register for your courses. This will be done in your Jagwire account. All registered courses must be on your degree plan. For any questions during registration or concerns about your degree plan, please get in touch with Academic Advising.
[Academic Advising](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/academic-affairs/index.html)
6. Use benefits at A&M-SA 
Military Affairs begins accepting benefit forms as soon as the semester registration window opens. There will be no documents collected unless all required documents are complete.
[Submit Benefit Request](https://bit.ly/3rzmICL)
