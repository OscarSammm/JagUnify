## Important Deadlines
##  October 1, FAFSA, TASFA and Scholarship Applications Open 
Complete these important applications as soon as they open in October. Certain types of financial aid are available on a first-come, first-served basis, so don't wait to apply!
##  January 15, FAFSA Priority Deadline 
To maximize your financial aid opportunity for the next year, we encourage you to submit your FAFSA or TASFA to Texas A&M University-San Antonio by this date.
##  Early April, Current Student Offers Posted & Summer Aid 
Award Notifications are sent to current students for the next academic year. Also, Summer Financial Aid awards begin in April.
##  June 30, General Scholarship Application Closes 
This is the final deadline! The Texas A&M University-San Antonio General Scholarship Application closes on June 30.
