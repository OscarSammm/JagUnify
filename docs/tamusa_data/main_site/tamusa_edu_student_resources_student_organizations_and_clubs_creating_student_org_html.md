## Creating a Student Organization
Please review all the current organizations in **[JagSync](https://jagsync.tamusa.edu)**. If there is an organization similar to the one you may want to start, please contact the organization before starting a new one. If you decide to create your organization, please follow the process below.
Starting an **[Interest Group](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/student-organizations-and-clubs/index.html)** :
  1. Visit the Student Involvement page in JagSync
  2. Fill out the [**Intent to Organize an Interest Group**](https://tamusa.campuslabs.com/engage/submitter/form/start/191647) Form
  3. The Student Life Coordinator will review your form and set up a portal on JagSync for you.


Starting a **[New Recognized Student Organization](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/student-organizations-and-clubs/index.html)**
  1. In JagSync, click on **[Organizations](https://tamusa.campuslabs.com/engage/organizations)** from the Explore Page
  2. Click on Register an Organization>Register a New Organization
  3. Follow all directions in that form
  4. You will need to have all requirements listed to become recognized
