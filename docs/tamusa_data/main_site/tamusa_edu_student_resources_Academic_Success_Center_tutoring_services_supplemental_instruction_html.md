## Welcome to Supplemental Instruction!
### What do we do?
SI Leaders are students that have already passed your class with a B or better and they host study sessions outside of class to cover difficult content: Be ready to study, have fun, and meet new people! 
### Supported Courses
Not sure if your course is supported by an SI leader? Look no further!
[SPRING 2025 SI Supported Courses](https://www.tamusa.edu/student-resources/Academic-Success-Center/tutoring-services/documents/SI-for-ALC-Website.pdf)
***Please keep in mind only students enrolled in a supported course may attend sessions as we have limited seating.***
