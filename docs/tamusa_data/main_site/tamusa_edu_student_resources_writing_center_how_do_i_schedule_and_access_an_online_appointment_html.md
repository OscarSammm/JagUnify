## How do I schedule and access an online appointment?
Online appointments with the Writing Center are easy to schedule and do not require you to download any additional software! Use our online scheduling system to make your appointment any time of the day!
1. Go into your **Jagwire** account, and click on the **Student Services** tab. Look under **Academic Support,** and click on “**Writing Center Appointment.** ”
![step one](https://www.tamusa.edu/academics/College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/images/step-one.png)
2. Find a tutor who has _“_**Online”** listed under their name and select a session time that works best for you. White squares show available times.
![step two](https://www.tamusa.edu/academics/College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/images/step-two.png)
3. Once you click on a white square, you can **upload documents** that you will be working on during your session. Be sure to **click the circle** that designates whether the appointment is **online** or **face-to-face**.
![step three](https://www.tamusa.edu/academics/College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/images/step-three.png)
4. Once you have set up your appointment, you can select the “**Create Appointment** ” button to officially save your appointment.
![step four](https://www.tamusa.edu/academics/College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/images/step-four.png)
5. Your appointment will appear in a **yellow square** once it has been saved. If you need to make any changes or sign in for your online session, just click on the yellow square.
![step five](https://www.tamusa.edu/academics/College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/images/step-five.png)
6. Once your appointment has been saved, you can **edit** or **cancel** the appointment at any time. When you are ready to start your online session, click “**Start Or Join Online Consultation.** ”
![step six](https://www.tamusa.edu/academics/College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/images/step-six.png)
7: 
  * When you start the online appointment, allow the program to access your **camera** and **microphone**. Both you and the tutor will appear on the far left of the screen. You can also text chat with your tutor on the far right in the **chat box**. 
  * To **upload a document,** click on the icon with two arrows, select the document from your computer, and click **upload.**
  * Your document will then appear in the **Whiteboard**. The center of the screen is the **Whiteboard** where you can work on your document with your tutor. The **editing tools** allow you to make changes to the document during your session.
  * Once your session is complete, simply exit out of the screen. All comments made during the session in the chat box, along with edits to the paper, can be accessed at any time by clicking on your appointment and reopening the session.


![step seven](https://www.tamusa.edu/academics/College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/images/step-seven.png)
