## Eligibility, Requirements & Award Nominations
**The Office of First-Year Experience** will celebrate the achievements of first-year students through a special Cording Ceremony, recognizing their dedication and engagement during their first year of college. Students who successfully earn a grade of C or higher in their First-Year Seminar and actively participate in at least two out of the four PROWL activities with a Peer Leader will be honored with a graduation cord and a certificate. This distinction highlights their commitment to academic success and campus involvement.
Additionally, a campus-wide request will be sent out for **nominations for first-year awards** , further acknowledging outstanding students who have demonstrated exceptional leadership, perseverance, and engagement within the First-Year Experience program. These awards aim to celebrate the diverse talents and contributions of first-year students, reinforcing the values of leadership, involvement, and academic success.
