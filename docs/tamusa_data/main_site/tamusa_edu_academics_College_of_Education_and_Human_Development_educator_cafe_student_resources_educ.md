## Educator Certification Recommendation Form
The following criteria must be met before submitting the Educator Certification Recommendation Form:
  1. Passed all required TExES exams for your certification area
  2. Successfully completed all coursework including clinical teaching (all education courses must be a "C" or better)
  3. Degree must be awarded and posted to your transcript


**Note: Once your Certification Recommendation Form has been processed, you will receive instruction on required fingerprinting.
