## Transfer Guides
**Welcome to the Department University Access Transfer Guides page**  
Transfer partnerships allow students to take the maximum number of courses possible before transferring with all college-level courses earned counting towards their intended major.   
  
Current transfer partners include:   
[Alamo Colleges ](http://myalamocatalog.alamo.edu/preview_entity.php?catoid=214&ent_oid=4789&returnto=15779 "Alamo Colleges")  
[Blinn College](https://www.blinn.edu/transfer-information/files/tamu/san-antonio/tamu-san-antonio-transfer-degree-map-and-guide.pdf "Blinn College")  
[Coastal Bend College](https://www.coastalbend.edu/student_success_engagement/student_services_accessibility/transfer_resources.html "Coastal Bend College")  
[Collin College](http://www.collin.edu/transferu/index.html "Collin College")  
[Laredo College](https://www.laredo.edu/enrollment-management/student-success-center/transfer-resources/index.html#:~:text=Transfer%20planning%20guides%20are%20a%20list%20of%20recommended,to%20a%20program%20of%20study%20at%20a%20university. "Laredo College")  
[Southwest Texas Junior College](https://www.swtxc.edu/current-students/transferring/tamusa-career-pathway.html "Southwest Texas Junior College")  
To follow one of these pathways, students must contact their advisor at the community college.
If you are a prospective transfer student, please contact the **[Texas A&M-San Antonio Office of Admissions](https://www.tamusa.edu/admissions/contact-us.html).**
