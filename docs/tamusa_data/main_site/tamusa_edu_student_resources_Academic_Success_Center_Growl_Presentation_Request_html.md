# Request a Presentation
Request a Presentation
We are available to speak to current Texas A&M University-San Antonio students and staff about our services or specific academic topics. A list of possible topics is below, but we can work with you to customize a presentation or workshop. Be sure to include the length of time you have available for the presentation.
Because of limited staff availability, the following conditions apply.
  * Limit is two presentations per semester per group. 
  * Must be affiliated with a Texas A&M University-San Antonio student organization or campus department/program.
  * Requests must be made at least 3 weeks prior to the event.
  * A staff member from the requesting group must be available during the presenting.
  * We require a minimum of 10 students in attendance for any presentation we give. We reserve the right to cancel a presentation request if this criteria is not met.


Questions about our presentations can be director to Michelle Anguiano at manguian@tamusa.edu. 
[Request a Presentation Form](https://tamusa.jotform.com/243405154157048)

## Topics
##  Overview of SASC Services 
15 minutes: What does the Student Academic Success Center do and how does it help students? What do Academic Coaches, Retention Specialists, and the Academic Learning center provide to help students be successful? This presenting provides and overview of the services of the SASC and how it can assist students in being success at Texas A&M University - San Antonio.
##  Textbook Reading 
30 minutes
Managing the sheer volume of college reading assignments, retaining critical information, and warding off distractions can be tough, especially for students in the first year. We provide tips on focus, the concentration cycle, and reading strategies so that students can maximize what they learn from reading.
