## Mission
The Department of Counseling, Health and Kinesiology is committed to extending the knowledge base of counseling, health and kinesiology through a combination of scholarly inquiry, reflective practices and student-centered learning. The faculty and staff are dedicated to preparing counselors and health/kinesiology professionals to meet the challenges of a growing and diverse community.
